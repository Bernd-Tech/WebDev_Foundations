What got me into Coding?

Because of an injury I had to retire from being a professional dancer 2 years ago. A journey of reconstructing my identity and finding something in which I can wholeheartedly invest myself in followed.

I tried to analyze what other skills I might have and could develope further. After realizing that I always had a great capacity for solving problems by deconstructing them into their smallest parts, I came to the conclusion that coding might be something that would suit me well.

So I started to watch some videos about the daily lives of software developers (not the idolized ones, but the authentic ones), did research about the potential of a career as a developer and started to follow tutorials on free code camp. I have been at it ever since and made it my goal to become highly skilled in it.

I don't know exactly in which field I would like to become an expert in yet, but as Rumi said "As you start to walk on the way, the way appears.". So I guess that's what I will do for now as best as I can.

Thrilled to be on this journey!
